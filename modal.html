Features

No confusing discounts. Just simple, transparent pricing.

Momentum Energy has ditched discounting in favour of delivering simple rates you can easily understand. With zero cheeky conditions and zero catches, it’s fair, transparent and simple.

Australian-based call centres.

Not only is Momentum 100% Australian owned and operated, their call centres are in Melbourne and Hobart. So if you ever need help, you don’t have to call overseas to get it.

Support renewable energy.

Momentum Energy is 100% owned by Hydro Tasmania – Australia’s largest renewable energy generator. Choose Momentum and you support clean Aussie renewables. Feels good, eh?

Get great customer service.

It’s not just Momentum saying this. In 2019, Canstar Blue gave them a 5-star rating for customer service in several categories and Mozo awarded them for Excellent Customer Service – all based on feedback from customers themselves.

Flexible payment options.

Choose a payment option that works for you. Momentum also offers monthly billing for electricity – a great way to stay in control of your budget. Just ask.

Online account management.

Pay your bills and update your details online.
